grief

    
"why do i bother being soft and full of feeling,
    when i am truly bitter and angry and full of grief"
    
    
    
    i loved her. i loved him. i loved them.
    whether in monogamy or in polyamory,
    i gave my entire heart to each of them.
    no matter how long i gave them my time,
    they each left a part of them within me.
    
    
    however,
    
    
    they were not always reciprocal in love.
    
    
    
    lies were told. promises broken.
    boundaries crossed. trust manipulated.
    texts ignored. secrets ensued.
    
    after every seemingly genuine apology,
    i stayed.
    
  
    
    but when they left,
    i sat alone in my thoughts.
    
    
    
    i asked myself,
     "what do i do with all this grief?"
    
    
    
    what did i do wrong? am i not enough?
    why did they leave me for her?
    
    
    how is it fair to hold all this grief on my own,
    when they are happy with their new partner?
    
    
    i wallowed in my grief for months. 
    i lost weight. i lost color in my flesh.
    i regained weight. i shaved my head.
    i got pierced (again). 
    
    
    
    but most importantly,
    i made art.
